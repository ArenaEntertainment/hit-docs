To play Casino games on Hit, first add funds to your Markets wallet using the standard deposit flow. Once the funds arrive, move the amount you want to use from your Markets wallet to your Casino wallet.

## Add Funds

Use the regular [Markets deposit flow](/markets/deposits) to add funds to your account.

You can deposit from the wallet dropdown:

1. Click your wallet balance at the top of the page.
2. Select **Manage Funds**.
3. Click **Deposit**.
4. Choose your deposit method.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions.

You can also deposit from your [portfolio](/markets/portfolio) page by clicking **Deposit**.

## Move Funds to Casino

After your deposit arrives in your Markets wallet, use **Move Funds** to transfer funds between your Markets wallet and Casino wallet.

You can find **Move Funds** in either place:

- The wallet dropdown.
- Your [portfolio](/markets/portfolio) page.

From there, choose the direction of the transfer:

| Direction | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Markets to Casino | Move funds into your Casino wallet to play Casino games. |
| Casino to Markets | Move funds back to your Markets wallet for trading or withdrawal. |

## Wallets

Your Markets wallet is your primary self-custodial Hit wallet for trading. It belongs to you, and you can export the private key from account settings.

Your Casino wallet is separate from your Markets wallet. Moving funds to Casino transfers the selected amount into the Casino wallet managed by the Casino experience.

Use [Moving Funds](/markets/moving-funds) for the shared transfer flow between Markets and Casino.
