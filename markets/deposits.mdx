To start trading on Hit, you'll need to deposit funds into your account.

### Deposit Methods

#### Crypto Transfer

**Deposit via QR Code:**  
Select your preferred network and token to generate a unique QR code (wallet address). Any funds sent to this address will be automatically converted and bridged to your Hit wallet. Use the estimation tool in the UI to review associated fees before confirming your transfer.

**Deposit from Your Connected Wallet:**  
If you have a wallet connected, you can select a supported token and seamlessly convert and bridge your desired amount directly into your Hit account.

**Direct transfer to your wallet address:**
You can also transfer Base USDC directly to your Hit wallet.

<Warning>
  **Important:** You must send USDC on the Base network only to your **PRIMARY**
  Hit wallet. Any other token, network or wallet address will result in
  permanent loss of funds.
</Warning>

#### Coinbase Onramp

Use Coinbase Onramp to:

- Deposit directly from your Coinbase account.
- Buy and deposit funds using your card (available in supported regions).

#### Moonpay

Use Moonpay to deposit funds directly from your debit or credit card (available in supported regions).

### How to Deposit

**From anywhere in the app:**

1. Click your wallet balance at the top of the page.
2. Select **Manage Funds**.
3. Click **Deposit**.
4. Choose your preferred deposit method.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your deposit.
6. Start trading once your deposit is confirmed.

**From your portfolio page:**

1. Click the **Deposit** button near the top.
2. Pick your deposit method.
3. Follow the on-screen prompts to finish.
4. Start trading when your deposit shows up in your account.
