To start trading on Hit, you'll need to deposit funds into your account. Before you start, make sure you have [created an account](/markets/signing-up).

<Info>
  Some deposit methods are only available in certain regions. Availability is
  determined by your location and the eligibility checks of our third-party
  providers, and is shown in the app at the time of deposit. Hit does not
  control which methods are available in your region, and you are responsible
  for ensuring your use of these methods complies with local laws and
  regulations.
</Info>

### Deposit Methods

#### QR Code

Select a network and token to generate a deposit address (shown as a QR code). Any supported funds sent to this address are automatically converted to USDC on the Base network and credited to your Hit wallet. Review the estimated fees shown in the app before confirming your transfer.

#### Connected Wallet

If you have a wallet connected, select a supported token and the amount you want to deposit. Hit will convert the funds to USDC on the Base network and credit them to your Hit account.

#### Direct Transfer

You can send USDC on the Base network directly to your primary Hit wallet. Your primary wallet is your self-custodial Markets wallet. You can find its address on your Profile page, and export the private key from account settings.

<Warning>
  **Important:** Only send USDC on the Base network, and only to your primary Hit wallet.
  - Sending a different token, or USDC on a different network, will result in permanent loss of funds.
  - Sending to any other address, including your signer wallet or an exchange deposit address, cannot be recovered by Hit support.
</Warning>

#### Coinbase Onramp

Deposit directly from your Coinbase account, or buy and deposit funds using your card.

#### Moonpay

Deposit funds directly from your debit or credit card.

### How to Deposit

Open the deposit flow from either of these entry points:

- Click your wallet balance at the top of any page, then select **Manage Funds → Deposit**.
- From your portfolio page, click the **Deposit** button near the top.

Then:

1. Choose your preferred deposit method.
2. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your deposit.
3. Start trading once your deposit is confirmed in your account.
