Hit rewards users who provide useful liquidity on eligible markets. Each eligible market draws from its own daily USDC pool. Post resting orders within the market's spread and size requirements and keep them live; while your liquidity qualifies, you earn a share of that market's pool.

Rewards accrue while your qualifying liquidity is live.

## The Reward Pool

Each eligible market has its own daily reward amount, set per market and shown in-product.

| Detail    | Value                            |
| :-------- | :------------------------------- |
| Pool size | Set per market, shown in-product |
| Currency  | USDC                             |
| Network   | Base                             |
| Cadence   | Daily                            |

A pool is a **ceiling for a market over a period, not a per-user entitlement**. The full amount may or may not be distributed.

<Note>
  Headline figures like "up to \$5,000" refer to campaign ceilings — not a
  per-market or per-user amount. Individual market pools may be much smaller.
</Note>

## How It Works

Post resting orders that make the market easier to trade, and you earn a share of that market's daily pool while they're live. In general:

- **Quote within the spread** — provide liquidity within the market's qualifying spread, shown in-product.
- **Meet the minimum size** — orders must meet the market's minimum size, shown in-product.
- **Stay live** — orders must rest on the book to count. Place-and-cancel activity does not earn.
- **Two-sided earns a boost** — one-sided liquidity earns, but quoting both sides earns more.

Each market's pool is shared among the users providing qualifying liquidity, based on how useful that liquidity is. Exact scoring metrics are not published and may change — the description here is high-level guidance only.

## Eligibility

To earn Liquidity Rewards:

- The market must have Liquidity Rewards turned on.
- Your resting orders must meet the market's spread and size requirements.
- Your orders must stay live long enough to be counted.

<Note>
  You must hold an eligible Hit account in good standing. Availability may be
  subject to regional and jurisdictional restrictions, and Hit may require
  identity or wallet verification before paying rewards.
</Note>
