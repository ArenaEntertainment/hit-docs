Hit rewards users who provide high-quality liquidity on selected markets.

Markets with liquidity rewards enabled will draw from a daily pool of USDC. If you provide two-way liquidity within a qualifying spread, your eligible size can earn a share of that market's rewards.

Rewards accrue minute by minute while your qualifying liquidity is live.

## How It Works

To be eligible for liquidity rewards:

- The market must have liquidity rewards turned on.
- You must provide liquidity on both sides of the market.
- Your orders must stay within a qualifying spread.

We do not publish the exact scoring metrics. In general, users who consistently provide useful two-way liquidity should be able to see rewards accrue over time.

Liquidity rewards are separate from the Hit Points program.
