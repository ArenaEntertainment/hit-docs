---
title: Moving Funds
description: Move funds between your Markets and Casino wallets
---

Use **Move Funds** to transfer funds between your Markets wallet and your Casino wallet.

Your Markets wallet is your primary self-custodial Hit wallet for trading. It belongs to you, and you can export the private key from account settings.

Your Casino wallet is separate from your Markets wallet. Moving funds to Casino transfers the selected amount into the Casino wallet managed by the Casino experience.

## Before You Move Funds

Deposit funds into your Markets wallet first:

1. Click your wallet balance at the top of the page.
2. Select **Manage Funds**.
3. Click **Deposit**.
4. Choose your deposit method.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions.

You can also deposit from your [portfolio](/markets/portfolio) page.

## Move Funds

You can open **Move Funds** from either place:

- The wallet dropdown.
- Your [portfolio](/markets/portfolio) page.

Then choose the direction:

| Direction | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Markets to Casino | Move funds into your Casino wallet to play Casino games. |
| Casino to Markets | Move funds back to your Markets wallet for trading or withdrawal. |

Use [Deposits](/markets/deposits) to add funds to your Markets wallet, and use [Withdrawals](/markets/withdrawals) to move funds out of Hit.
