You can withdraw your funds from Hit at any time. Before you start, make sure you have a withdrawable balance in your Hit account.

### Withdrawal Methods

#### Crypto Wallet

Withdraw to any wallet that supports USDC on the Base network.

<Warning>
  **Important:**
  - You must provide a wallet address that supports USDC on the Base network. Sending to any other network or asset will result in permanent loss of funds.
  - Exercise caution when withdrawing directly to exchange wallets; not all exchanges support Base USDC deposits.
</Warning>

#### Fiat Withdrawals

Depending on your region, additional methods such as withdrawing to a linked Coinbase account or bank may be offered. Availability is determined by your location and the eligibility checks of our third-party providers, and is shown in the app at the time of withdrawal. Hit does not control which methods are available in your region, and you are responsible for ensuring your use of these methods complies with local laws and regulations.

### How to Withdraw

1. Click the **Withdraw** button on the portfolio page.
2. Choose your preferred withdrawal method.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions.

<Info>
  **Limit Orders:** If you have resting limit orders, withdrawing funds may
  cause some orders to be cancelled if fulfilling them would exceed your
  available liquid balance.
</Info>
