Your portfolio is the central hub for managing your trading activity on Hit.

### Overview

At the top of your portfolio, you'll see:

- **Balance** - Your wallet total balance, with cash and open positions shown separately.
- **Deposit** - Add funds to your wallet.
- **Withdraw** - Withdraw funds from your wallet.

### Portfolio Tabs

#### Market Positions

View all your active market positions with details including:

- **Market** - The prediction market name.
- **Avg > Now** - Your average purchase price compared to current market price.
- **Bet** - Total cost of the position when opened.
- **To Win** - Potential payout if your position wins.
- **Value** - Current value of your position.

From the Market Positions tab, you can:

- **Sell positions** - Sell your shares before the market resolves.
- **Redeem winning positions** - Claim your winnings after a market resolves in your favor.
- **Remove losing positions** - Clear resolved positions from your portfolio.

#### Market Orders

View and manage your open limit orders. You can cancel any resting orders from this tab.

#### Market History

Review your complete trading history.

#### Funds

Review your funding activity, including deposits, withdrawals and transfers between wallets.
