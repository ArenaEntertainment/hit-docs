The Hit leaderboard will showcase top traders and create friendly competition within the community.

## Current Status

We are currently in Season 0. The leaderboard is not yet live.

From Season 1 onwards, traders will be able to:

- View their ranking.
- See top performers.
- Compete for recognition.
- Track their progress.

## How It Works

The leaderboard will rank traders based on their [points](/users/points) earned through platform activity.

Follow [@Hit](https://x.com/Hit) for updates on Season 1 and the leaderboard launch.
